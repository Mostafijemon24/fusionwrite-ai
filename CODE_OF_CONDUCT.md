# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all members of the fusionwrite-ai community. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all participants and the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

## Our Community Standards

### 1. Be Respectful
- Treat all community members with respect and dignity
- Respect different opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Listen actively and thoughtfully to others
- Acknowledge and respect cultural and personal differences

### 2. Be Inclusive
- Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities
- Actively work to make spaces accessible and inclusive
- Encourage participation from underrepresented groups
- Be mindful of language that may be exclusive or harmful

### 3. Be Collaborative
- Work together toward shared goals
- Share knowledge and help others learn
- Provide constructive feedback in a supportive manner
- Celebrate contributions and achievements of community members

### 4. Be Professional
- Keep discussions professional and on-topic
- Avoid personal attacks or insults
- Disagree respectfully about ideas, not about people
- Take disputes offline if they become unproductive

### 5. Be Responsible
- Take responsibility for your words and actions
- Report issues through appropriate channels
- Support the enforcement of this Code of Conduct
- Help maintain a safe and welcoming community

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated in the fusionwrite-ai community:

- **Harassment**: Unwelcome comments or conduct based on protected characteristics (race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, national origin, age, etc.)
- **Discrimination**: Treating individuals unfairly based on their identity or characteristics
- **Bullying**: Intimidation, threats, or hostile behavior toward other community members
- **Hate Speech**: Language that attacks or demeans individuals or groups
- **Sexual Harassment**: Unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, or other sexually explicit conduct
- **Spam**: Excessive self-promotion, off-topic posts, or unsolicited advertising
- **Disruptive Behavior**: Actions that disrupt discussions or project work
- **Doxing**: Sharing personal information without consent
- **Trolling**: Deliberately provoking conflict or offense
- **Violation of Privacy**: Sharing others' private information without permission

## Reporting and Enforcement

### How to Report
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Direct Communication**: Contact the person directly if you feel safe doing so
2. **Project Maintainers**: Email the project maintainers with details of the incident
3. **Escalation**: For severe issues, contact the repository owner directly

When reporting, please include:
- A description of the incident
- Who was involved
- When and where it occurred
- Any witnesses or evidence
- Your contact information

### Investigation and Response

Upon receiving a report, the project maintainers will:

1. Acknowledge receipt of your report promptly
2. Investigate the complaint thoroughly and impartially
3. Maintain the confidentiality of the reporter, unless they consent to disclosure
4. Communicate findings and actions to the involved parties
5. Take appropriate corrective action when necessary

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- **Warning**: A private or public warning about the behavior
- **Temporary Suspension**: Temporary removal from community spaces
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the project and related spaces
- **Legal Action**: For severe cases, appropriate legal measures may be taken

The severity of consequences will be determined based on the nature and frequency of the violation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and code
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Discussion forums and chat channels
- Project events and gatherings
- Any official communication channels
- Interactions representing the project in public spaces

## Community Spaces

All participants in community spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, etc.) are expected to uphold this Code of Conduct.

## Acknowledgment

By participating in the fusionwrite-ai community, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.

## Changes to This Code of Conduct

We may update this Code of Conduct periodically to reflect the evolving needs of our community. All updates will be communicated to community members, and continued participation constitutes acceptance of the updated version.
